Why We Built This

A personal problem that needed a better solution.

Money is the #1 source of stress in relationships. Studies show 35% of couples say finances cause the most friction in their partnership. Yet most finance apps are built for individuals — they ignore the reality that couples share expenses, save together, and make financial decisions as a team.

We built Build Together because we couldn't find an app that treated shared finances as a collaboration instead of a chore. One that didn't require handing over bank credentials. One that gently helped instead of shamed.

Built for Two

Every feature is designed for partners managing money together, not retrofitted from a single-user app.

Privacy by Design

We chose no bank linking on purpose. You control what goes in. Your data is never sold or shared.

Encouragement, Not Guilt

Behavioral science says shame doesn't work. Our AI adapts to what actually motivates you.
